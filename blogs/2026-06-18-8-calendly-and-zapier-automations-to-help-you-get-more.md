This Calendly blog post presents eight workflow automations that combine Calendly with Zapier to boost productivity. The integrations automatically sync booking data to Google Sheets, update CRM records in tools like Pipedrive, schedule meetings from form submissions, create task management entries, and keep Airtable and Notion databases synchronized with meeting activity.
